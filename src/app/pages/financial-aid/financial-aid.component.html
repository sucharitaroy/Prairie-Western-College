Financial Aid

Colleges can be very expensive. Government recognizes the advantages of post-secondary education as well as the struggle for individuals to pay for their post-secondary education. Many types of funding are offered and you may be eligible for those.

There are different types of facilities available

  • Scholarships and awards
  • Bursaries
  • Grants

Alberta Student Aid offers student loans, grants, scholarships, and awards for Albertians pursuing full-time or part-time post-secondary studies.

The Financial Aid co-ordination team of Prairie Western College is dedicated to answering your various questions.

Please contact us (via phone 403-454-0948 or via email informaion.pwc) to talk to one of our experienced co-ordinators to discuss your situation confidentially. You are not alone in this journey.
