Why Choose Us

  • Our programs encompass all aspects of business to make our graduates successful in real world.

  • The programs are taught by highly educated North American and European graduates who have vast industry experience on global setting.

  • We offer flexible class time (week days, evening and week-end) with affordable tuition fees.

  • Scholarship, loan and grants are available for eligible students

  • Career counseling is integral part of our programs. Students will have easy access to our career counselor.

  • Easy access to public transportation – just 5 minutes walking distance from Marlborough LRT station
